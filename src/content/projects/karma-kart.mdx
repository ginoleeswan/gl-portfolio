approach: A barcode scanner that resolves a product to its parent company and returns a personalized Karma Score across eight values, on one Expo SDK 55 codebase, enriched by Gemini in a Supabase Edge Function.
decisions:
  - icon: "lucide:calculator"
    title: Personal score, testable math
    body: An onboarding quiz sets per-value weights; the score normalizes them against company pillar scores. The algorithm lives in a pure lib/ module, so it runs under Jest in milliseconds.
  - icon: "lucide:credit-card"
    title: Payments split by platform
    body: RevenueCat on native to satisfy Apple and Google IAP rules, Paddle on web as merchant of record. One subscription, two payment paths.
  - icon: "lucide:server"
    title: AI key server-side only
    body: Gemini runs inside the Edge Function with the key stored as a Supabase secret, never in the app bundle.
challenge: Barcode databases are full of gaps and conflicts, so product lookup is a ranked waterfall across five sources, with Gemini handling enrichment rather than being the source of truth.
